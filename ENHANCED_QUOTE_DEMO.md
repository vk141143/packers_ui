# Enhanced Quote Calculator Demo

## Overview

The Enhanced Quote Calculator Demo showcases a comprehensive booking form integrated with an advanced quote calculation service. This demo demonstrates the complete workflow from customer input to detailed pricing breakdown.

## Features

### Enhanced Booking Form
- **5-Step Process**: Streamlined multi-step form for comprehensive data collection
- **Contact Information**: Full customer details with preferred contact methods
- **Property Details**: Detailed property information including access restrictions
- **Service Requirements**: Comprehensive service selection with special item handling
- **Booking Details**: Flexible scheduling with date/time preferences
- **Additional Information**: Photo uploads and special instructions

### Quote Calculator Service
- **Smart Pricing**: Advanced algorithms considering multiple factors
- **Detailed Breakdown**: Itemized pricing with category-based organization
- **Dynamic Calculations**: Real-time price adjustments based on requirements
- **SLA Compliance**: Different pricing tiers for urgency levels
- **Access Difficulty**: Pricing adjustments for property access challenges
- **Special Items**: Additional charges for heavy, fragile, or hazardous items

## Quote Calculation Factors

The calculator considers:
- **Base Service Rates**: Different rates per service type
- **Volume Multipliers**: Pricing based on estimated volume
- **Urgency Surcharges**: Emergency and urgent service premiums
- **Access Difficulty**: Parking and property access challenges
- **Special Items**: Heavy, fragile, and hazardous item handling
- **Additional Services**: Packing and storage service charges
- **Distance Charges**: Location-based pricing adjustments
- **Flexible Date Discounts**: Savings for flexible scheduling
